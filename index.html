An extension of an energy experiment.

I spend my days looking after protection relays and system observability devices (fault recorders, phasor measurement units and the like) at SSEN Transmission. I've always had an active interest in the energy landscape in the UK. Combined with that, I've had a passing interest in programming since my university days. Combining all that together, my interest was peaked by GB Renewables Map by Robin Hawkes, and wondered what would be involved in replicating Robin's work (upto a point) and extending it to include the full mix of generation in the UK. This is the result of these musings.

A couple of notes:

Data Sources

  • Generator Balancing Mechanism Unit and output data is derived from the Elexon Insights Solution
  • Generator location data is derived from the Digest of UK Energy Statistics (DUKES), specifically table 5.11
  • Matching of BMU data to generator location data is done by me...
  • Maps by maplibre and maptiler

The data presented are generator outputs as stated in the Physical Notice for the current settlement period. This is the closest offering from the Elexon API to a "real time" output. Not sure how constraints are managed, and something I will explore in future.

Final thoughts (for now!)

Please enjoy! Any comments would be appreciated. Future revisions of the map may include (time permitting) the ability to examine network output during a user selected settlement period.
